# Question Bank

## General Work

- What outcome should exist when this task is done?
- Who will use or review the result?
- What format should the final deliverable use?
- What constraints, tone, deadline, or decision criteria matter?

## Research Work

- What decision should the research support?
- Which source types are acceptable or unacceptable?
- How current does the evidence need to be?
- Should the output optimize for breadth, depth, or a recommendation?

## Coding Work

- Is this a demo, MVP, production change, bugfix, refactor, or learning task?
- What is in scope and out of scope for this session?
- What commands prove the work is correct?
- What files, APIs, or user flows are most relevant?
- What should the agent avoid touching?
